  • leaves: n
  • levels: logn+1
  • height: logn
  • T(n) = cmerge ⋅ n
  • merge is in ϴ(n), work per level is in ϴ(n)
  • work total ≈ ϴ(n)(logn+1), because logn+1 layers
  • T(n) = nlogn+n
